W13: ISCCC for Low-Altitude Intelligent Networking

VTC-Fall 2025 Workshop

Call for Papers

The rapid growth of low-altitude intelligent networks, driven by UAVs, aerial mobility, and smart infrastructure, demands the synergistic integration of sensing, communication, computing, and control (ISCCC). This workshop explores innovations and solutions for ISCCC in aerial environments, addressing challenges in real-time processing, decision-making, spectrum usage, and security.

Topics of Interest
